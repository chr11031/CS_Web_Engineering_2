Welcome to the Ranch

My story

My name is Dane Christensen and I love writing software. I am from Boston Massachussetts and from a young age had a fascination with computers. When I was 18 I discovered my potential when I began to play around with coding websites using HTML, PHP, and MySQL. I made it a personal challenge to myself to make a small social networking site for myself. I've been coding ever since. After discovering my passion for coding I started to learn about C++, Java, and a variety of other related technologies. Although I now program primarily in C++, I am happy to work on a website that will offer further programming experience for the web end where I began.

Why Keyboard Cowboys?

The term comes from the movie "Hackers" produced in 1995. Although the movie is harder to take seriously than a comedy sketch, it offered one reference to the new generation of hackers as being "Keyboard Cowboys." I liked the idea and started to work on the site. I hope this website will evolve and lend itself as an effective software development resource.
